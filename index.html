A little about Me..

Montana grown.

Portland living.

I started out in Portland four years ago without a path. I came here to find myself and start over. I decided to go back to school in 2013 at PCC Sylvania. I saw that they had a website designing program. I had no experience, but have always been computer savvy and creative. I didn't know what to expect my first day, but finally found my niche. I fell in love with coding and continued until I graduated with an AAS degree. The last year, I found myself back in an industry I was trying to get out of before. So I decided to come to Epicodus and continue to follow my passion for coding!
